HOW SAFE ARE WE ONLINE?
  • Online safety is the ability to understand and recognize threats that exist on the internet, as well as having the skills and knowledge to avoid these threats. This includes knowing how to keep personal information private and secure online, protecting devices from malware, avoiding harmful or illegal content, and managing online relationships safely.

When it comes to the internet, safety is always a concern. Whether you use the Internet for work, online shopping, or for simply browsing the web, it’s important to take precautions to protect yourself from scammers and cybercriminals

 

Your personal information is valuable. It can be used to steal your identity, open new lines of credit in your name, and drain your bank accounts. Protecting your personal information is essential to protecting your finances and your good name.

There is no "completely secure" Internet

 

The thing is, no one is ever completely secure online, and while you don’t think you’ve been hacked, there’s an increasingly likely chance some of your personal information has been or will be compromised by a data breach the more services or sites you use or visit online.
